Tutorial and Overview

In this HIT, you are going to read 2 news articles on scientific research.

For each article, you will do three tasks.
  • (1) Reading + Questioning

    Read the article and raise five or more questions. Here question can be about what you want while reading the article.

  • (2) Quiz Making

    Based on the article, make a quiz with five or more questions. Select some sentences with important information and generate quiz question that can be answered by the selected sentence(s). You may suppose you are a teacher and making a quiz for your student to check whether they grasp important information from the article.

  • (3) Survey on Task

    Survey on the article itself and the previous two steps.

Below is an overview of this HIT. This will take 25 to 35 minutes.
